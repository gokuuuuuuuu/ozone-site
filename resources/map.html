NGSIM

United States 4 sites

Vehicle trajectory data collected from overhead cameras at four arterial and freeway sites across the United States. Each location includes lane-level geometry, signal timing, and synchronized trajectory records at 10 Hz. Widely used for car-following, lane-change, and microsimulation model calibration.
